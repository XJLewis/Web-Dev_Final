What is Baldur's Gate 3?

Baldur's Gate 3 is an RPG set in the Dungeons & Dragons universe, developed by Larian Studios. It follows the story of a group of adventurers who become infected with a mysterious parasitic tadpole that grants them dangerous powers. As they seek a cure, they uncover a plot involving dark forces that threaten the world, including an invasion by illithids (mind flayers) and a clash between gods and mortals. The game is known for its deep storytelling, character-driven narrative, and extensive player choices. It features a dynamic combat system based on Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition rules, with turn-based combat and a strong emphasis on strategic planning. Players can choose from a variety of races, classes, and backgrounds for their characters, and interact with NPCs, forge alliances, or make enemies based on their decisions.


What is this website?

This website is a companion to your Baldur's Gate 3 party. With it, you will be able to track simple stats and inventories of all the characters in your party or in camp. You can add and delete characters to your party and manage their inventories with this simple webapp.

Why Use This Website?

Our website makes managing your Baldur's Gate 3 characters and inventory easier than ever. With features like seamless party management, character customization, and quick inventory tracking, you'll never lose track of your progress again.
